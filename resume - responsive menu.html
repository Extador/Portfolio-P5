Bio

San Jose Based Digital Media artist has been honing his craft in the physical and digital mediums of art for years to create art that boggles expectations and inspires creativity. Initially a student in photography, Kyle Switched to digital art mediums like illustration and video editing to blend his teachings of the arts into a flexible format where he could accurately express his creativity. Kyle’s union of merging digital art techniques and physical studio work have helped to develop his style into a fluid artform that bends mediums and genres together in a playful and harmonious way. His use of color, symbolism and metaphors in his work are standout defining qualities of his art that give life and meaning to each piece. Kyle hopes to develop his art into digital advertising in his early career with the main goal of working in marketing, web design or video editing
